the DC Music Project

dcmusicproject.org

In celebration of the District and the first annual Funk Parade, this is the DC Music Project. In the coming months, we'll build a giant collection of people's memories of the city's music and what it has meant to all of us.

Get involved! Here are 3 ways to make this thing incredible.

[ONE] Tell your story! What was the first time you heard music performed? What was your most memorable show? Tell us your story. We'll put it on a map, share it in an app, and use it to build a people's history.

[TWO] Volunteer! Come to the next Code for DC meetup on May 14th and help put these stories on the web. We need coders, writers, graphic designers, musicians, historians, ...you get the idea. Everyone has something to contribute. Don't be shy now.

[THREE] Partner! Are you an artist or a venue owner? We'd love to add your shows. Are you a librarian or journalist? Let's add your archive to the site.
