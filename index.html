the DC Music Project

dcmusicproject.org

In celebration of the District and the Funk Parade, this is the DC Music Project. We're building a people's history of the city's music and what it means to all of us.

The full site will launch in the comming weeks with everyone's stories for the world to see.

Get involved! Here are 3 ways to make this thing incredible.

[ONE] Tell us your story. What was the first time you heard music performed? What was your most memorable show?

[TWO] Volunteer! Come to the next Code for DC meetup and help put these stories on the web. We need coders, writers, graphic designers, musicians, historians, ...you get the idea.

[THREE] Partner! Are you an artist or a venue owner? We'd love to add your shows. Are you a librarian or journalist? Let's add your archive to the site. Get in touch!
